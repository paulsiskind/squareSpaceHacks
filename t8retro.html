Product Summary

Amatis Retrofit Kits with Advanced Wireless Controls offer all the energy savings of a standard LED upgrade and more. Embedded Amatis 6LoWirelessTM controls allow customers and installers to program a turn-key LED lighting system with occupancy timeouts, daylight harvesting, detailed scheduling, and smooth dimming profiles in order to comply with local codes.


Amatis sensors and switches are designed for fast installation. Wireless commissioning of devices and lighting zones is quick and simple with the Amatis smartphone app saving valuable time.




Advanced Wireless Lighting Controls

The Amatis smart lighting solution has all the benefits of a wireless control system made easy to install and affordable. Amatis motion and light sensors are designed for plug-and- play installation and our wireless switches can be installed virtually anywhere.
The Amatis Border Router (AMBR) can communicate wirelessly with up to 200 devices, monitoring the system and uploading real-time data to our Lighting Dashboard.
Smart occupancy settings allow for different light levels at different times of the day, enabling additional energy savings.

Control & Commissioning APP

Amatis 6LoWireless devices can all be individually addressed and programmed with our easy to use smartphone app.


The app allows users to set lighting zones, occupancy timeouts, daylight settings, and program detailed schedules.


The app can be used anywhere with an internet connection, providing users with 24/7 oversight of their lighting system usage and behavior.

Real-Time Dashboard

Customers recieve reports on energy savings, ROI tracking, and environmental data via our simple user interface. Our powerful graphing engine also provides the ability to customize the dashboard for specific data requirements. The Amatis cloud-based API also allows users to export data for further analysis and third-party system integrations.
